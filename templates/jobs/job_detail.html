{# ── How Modal (Duplo BC Reader) ── #}

How to Change Barcode Reading — Duplo DC-646

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Open Select Menu
    Press F to open the Select Menu
  2. Navigate to Settings
    Use ↑ / ↓ to highlight Settings2, then press SET
  3. Toggle Barcode Reading
    Use ← / → to toggle Read Barcode to ON or OFF
  4. Advance Settings
    Press ↓ to advance to the next setting
  5. Save Changes
    Press SET to save. Changes are lost if you exit without pressing SET.

Barcode Reading Status:

  • ON — Machine auto-reads barcode and selects the matching JOB
  • OFF — JOB must be selected manually from the control panel
  • BC appears on the standby screen when barcode reading is active

Barcode Requirements:

Must be CODE-39 format, positioned 3–20 mm from lead edge and 25–60 mm from right edge.
